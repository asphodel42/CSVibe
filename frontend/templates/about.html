About CSVibe

CSVibe is the easiest way to turn raw CSV data into beautiful, interactive charts—in seconds. Simply upload your file (or drag & drop), and asynchronous, multi-threaded backend will crunch the numbers while you watch real-time status updates. When it’s done, explore your data with a fully interactive Plotly.js scattergl chart and key summary statistics.

My Mission

I believe in making data visualization fast, private, and accessible to everyone. Whether you’re a data scientist doing a quick sanity check or a business user preparing a presentation, CSVibe gets you from raw numbers to clear insights without installing heavy software.

Key Features

  • Asynchronous CSV processing with real-time progress feedback
  • Automatic calculation of mean, median, min & max values
  • Interactive Plotly.js charts (scattergl) with built-in hover details
  • Drag & drop or “Choose File” upload—no account or installation required
  • Download your chart as a PNG in one click
  • 100% private—your data never leaves our server (or your device, if you self-host)

Future Roadmap

I’m working on adding support for multiple series, additional chart types, and deeper statistical analyses (standard deviation, quartiles, custom aggregation intervals). Stay tuned!
